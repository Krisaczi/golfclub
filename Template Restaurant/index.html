About Us

Our Golf Resort

Royal Masovian Golf Club boasts an 18-hole course that’s considered one of the most challenging in Europe. The first 11 holes feature a park-like setting with wide fairways and picturesque lakes. As you progress, the last 7 holes become narrower, surrounded by lush forests. It’s not just about skill; strategic play is key to achieving a good score here.

In the early 19th century, a group of aristocrats and avid golf enthusiasts gathered under the ancient oak tree on Duke John’s estate. Inspired by the rolling hills and serene lakes, they envisioned a place where gentlemen could hone their skills and enjoy spirited matches.

Our History

Relax

By the late 1800s, the Royal Oakshire Golf Club had become a hub for social gatherings. Ladies’ tea parties, croquet matches, and moonlit dances graced its elegant clubhouse. Yet, amidst the laughter and camaraderie, the spirit of golf endured—a timeless pursuit that connected generations across the centuries.
